#### 1. Node Autonomy
The Distributed Meditation Network (DMN) provides infrastructure and protocols only. Each node is an autonomous, self-governing entity.
* **No Central Authority**: DMN does not employ facilitators or own physical locations.
* **Local Responsibility**: Node facilitators are responsible for complying with their local laws and safety regulations.

#### 2. The Peer Structure (No-Guru Policy)
By using this infrastructure, you agree that:
* **The Protocol is the Teacher**: No individual shall be elevated as a "guru" or permanent leader.
* **Rotating Facilitation**: Roles must rotate among peers to prevent the formation of hierarchy.

#### 3. Non-Commercial Use
The **Heart Protocol** and DMN infrastructure are open-source. They are intended for the advancement of collective coherence and must not be sold or gatekept for private profit.

#### 4. Research Disclaimer
This is an alpha-stage (v1.0) project. Participation is voluntary and intended for research purposes regarding the **2027 Coherence Window**.
